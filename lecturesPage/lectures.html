NASA Climate Kids

(https://climatekids.nasa.gov)

The NASA Climate Kids site gives kids the ability to be able to comprehend the meaning of climate change, and its outcomes on the Earth in a fun way. The website encourages kids to learn about climate change with fun games, videos, and activities which makes the topic not only engaging, but also educative for kids.

Kids Against Climate Change

(https://kidsagainstclimatechange.co)

Kids Against Climate Change really helps kids to understand the idea of climate change and its effects on our planet, and on how they can help. The variety of resources on this website gives kids a good understanding of how we are being impacted by current climate and weather conditions.

National Geographic Kids' Climate Change

(https://kids.nationalgeographic.com/science/article/climate-change)

The Kids National Geographic site provides to kids information about current climate conditions by providing history on how climate change has impacted our ecosystems, and what we can do about it (now in the present). Like the NASA site it also provides videos and games to keep things interactive and fun.
