NASA Climate Kids

(https://climatekids.nasa.gov)

NASA Climate Kids gives kids the ability to be able to comprehend the meaning of climate change, and its outcomes on the Earth in a fun way. The website encourages kids to learn about climate change with games, videos, and activities to do that make climate change available and engaging to work with in education for kids.

Kids Against Climate Change

(https://kidsagainstclimatechange.co)

Kids Against Climate Change really helps kids to understand the idea of climate change and its effects on the planet, and what kids can do to help. It gives kids a good understanding by its variety of resources to use from different sources.

National Geographic Kids' Climate Change

(https://kids.nationalgeographic.com/science/article/climate-change)

National Geographic Kids' Climate Change provides to kids information about climate change by telling you what it is, what has changed since the past, and what we can do about it (now in the present). It tells kids in an engaging way by making games, videos, and exploration which is educational and fun at the same time.
