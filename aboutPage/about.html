ABOUT CLIMATE CONVERT

Right now, somewhere on Earth, there is a climate crisis! I mean, can you believe it? We see the effects of it on our standards of living!
Climate Convert is all about introducing kids to our current climate situation. We feel that if young people learn and know about the history of climate change and the current climate situation on Earth, then they'll seek to create positive changes in their environment, which will subsequently make for a more climate-healthy Earth!
Are you ready to start learning? We hope so!
