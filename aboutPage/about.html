ABOUT CLIMATE CONVERT

Right now, somewhere on Earth there is a climate crisis! I mean, can you believe it? We see the effects of it in our standards of living! Climate Convert is all about introducing kids to the current climate situation on Earth. We feel that if young ones learn and know about the climate crisis on Earth, then they will seek to create positive changes in our Earth's environment. Every positive change will make for a climate-healthy Earth! You might wonder why you should care, and the answer is: Well, duh - its our planet's future! I'm sure everyone's got like a favorite flower, or something they like about nature, and if you don't want it to go away then, you better start learning! Are you ready? I mean, I hope so!
